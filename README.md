## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

To utilise the portal, an account must be registered.

1. Navigate to the portal homepage.
2. Click **Register**.
3. Enter email address and password of choice.
4. Click **Submit** to create account.

> **Note:** Each email address can only be associated with one account.

### Logging In

1. Navigate to the portal homepage.
2. Click **Login**.
3. Enter registered email address and password.
4. Click **Submit** to access dashboard. 

### Dashboard

Once logged in, the dashboard displays all created experiments. From here, you can:

- Create and stage a new experiment
- Resume an experiment in progress
- View previously uploaded datasets and their associated results
- Delete experiments no longer needed

> **Note:** All data in the portal is private to your account. You cannot view or modify experiments from other users.
